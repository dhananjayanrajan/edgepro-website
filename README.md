# EdgePro

Premium trading execution tools built around clarity, discipline, and directional structure.

## The Product

**DART Bias Engine** is a private, invite-only TradingView indicator that converts complex multi-timeframe market pressure into a single, clean visual output: bullish, bearish, or about to flip. It paints the chart background, marks confirmed bias transitions with flip labels, and fires alerts on directional state changes. No parameters to configure. No curve-fitting. Just a locked bias engine that gives traders a directional anchor before they risk capital.

DART solves the core problem professional traders face daily: five overlapping indicators, each technically correct in isolation, together creating decision paralysis. Every hesitation is a missed entry or a bad one. DART replaces that noise with one answer.

## The Platform

EdgePro is the sales and delivery surface for DART and upcoming MT5 automation bots. It handles the full customer lifecycle:

- Product discovery through a high-conversion marketing funnel
- Plan selection and Stripe checkout
- TradingView username collection for invite-only access delivery
- Wallet deposits across ETH, BSC, and Polygon networks
- MT5 account credential management for bot license generation
- Subscription tracking and license file distribution

## Visual Language

Dark cyberpunk aesthetic with glassmorphism layers, neon accent glows, and heavy motion design. The interface mirrors the feel of a live trading terminal while maintaining the clarity of a focused marketing funnel. Every animation and glass panel serves the singular goal of conversion.

## Template Scope

This is a static frontend template. All interactive elements, dashboards, wallet UI, and checkout flows are fully designed and animated. It is backend-agnostic and ready to connect to any API, CMS, or Web3 infrastructure.
